Plinko ⚪

Set your bet and drop the ball.
Goal: Drop a ball through the pegs. It bounces randomly left and right and lands in a slot worth a multiplier of your bet. Center slots are common but pay little (0.5×); the outer edges are rare jackpots (up to 9×). Drop several balls at once to spread your luck. You can't aim — that's the point.
